# 🛒 IndiaKart E-Commerce Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **E-Commerce Analytics solution** developed using **Python and Power BI**. The project focuses on transforming raw e-commerce data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), KPI analysis, and interactive dashboard development.

The analysis covers key business areas including **revenue performance, customer behavior, category performance, operations, and inventory management**, enabling data-driven decision-making across different areas of the business.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall revenue and business performance.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior and customer trends.
- Evaluate product category performance and revenue contribution.
- Monitor order and operational performance.
- Identify inventory risks and stock-related issues.
- Track key business KPIs and performance trends.
- Develop interactive dashboards for business monitoring and decision-making.

## 🔄 Project Workflow

The project was completed in four major phases:

### 1️⃣ Phase 1 – Data Cleaning

Performed data preparation and cleaning using Python to ensure data quality before analysis.

Key activities included:

- Understanding and validating multiple interconnected datasets.
- Identifying and handling missing values and duplicates.
- Correcting data types and inconsistencies.
- Preparing cleaned datasets for exploratory and business analysis.

📁 `Phase1_DataCleaning_Navdeep Khandelwal.ipynb`

---

### 2️⃣ Phase 2 – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Performed exploratory data analysis using Python to understand patterns, distributions, relationships, and trends within the e-commerce data.

The analysis focused on:

- Revenue and sales trends
- Customer behavior
- Category performance
- Order and operational patterns
- Inventory-related trends

📁 `Phase2_EDA_Navdeep Khandelwal.ipynb`

---

### 3️⃣ Phase 3 – KPI & Business Analysis

Analyzed key business metrics and KPIs to evaluate the overall performance of IndiaKart.

The analysis included:

- Revenue performance
- Customer metrics
- Category-level performance
- Operational KPIs
- Inventory indicators
- Business performance trends

📁 `Phase3_KPIs_Navdeep Khandelwal.ipynb`

---

### 4️⃣ Phase 4 – Power BI Dashboard & Business Reporting

Developed an interactive **5-page Power BI report** to present business insights across different analytical perspectives.

The dashboards provide dedicated views for:

1. Revenue Overview

3. Category Performance
